Deaf and Hard of Hearing Patient Care Simulation

An interactive training experience for healthcare professionals

Welcome to the Training Simulation

This interactive training will guide you through various scenarios involving healthcare interactions with Sandra Wilson, a deaf patient who communicates using American Sign Language (ASL).

Through these scenarios, you'll learn best practices for:

  • Communicating effectively with deaf patients
  • Setting up and troubleshooting interpretation services
  • Adapting medical consultations to ensure accessibility
  • Managing emergency situations with communication barriers
  • Creating comprehensive follow-up plans

Your decisions throughout the simulation will affect the patient experience and your overall performance assessment.
